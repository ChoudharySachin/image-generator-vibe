# TUTERO AI IMAGES - STYLE GUIDE & PROMPT TEMPLATE

## Visual Style Analysis (Based on Reference Images)

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Style**: 3D rendered, friendly character-based illustration
- **Tone**: Warm, approachable, helpful, encouraging
- **Setting**: Educational context with technology integration
- **Character**: Friendly robot/AI assistant as central figure

### The Tutero AI Character
- **Design**: Rounded, friendly robot with expressive features
- **Color**: Teal/turquoise primary color (#4ECDC4, #45B7D1)
- **Features**: 
  - Large, friendly eyes or screen face
  - Rounded body shape (non-threatening)
  - Floating/hovering appearance
  - Glowing or illuminated elements
  - Antenna or tech details
- **Expression**: Helpful, encouraging, intelligent
- **Size**: Prominent but not overwhelming (30-40% of composition)

### Student Representation
- **Style**: Diverse, relatable, modern students
- **Age Range**: Middle school to high school (12-17 years)
- **Diversity**: Various ethnicities, genders, appearances
- **Expression**: Engaged, curious, learning
- **Clothing**: Casual, contemporary school attire
- **Interaction**: Looking at device/screen, engaging with AI

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Teal/turquoise (#4ECDC4, #45B7D1) - AI character
- **Secondary**: Soft purple/lavender (#9B59B6, #B19CD9) - accents
- **Tertiary**: Warm coral/peach (#FF6B6B, #FFB6B6) - highlights
- **Background**: Light cream/off-white (#FFF9F5, #FFFBF7)
- **Tech Elements**: Soft blue glow (#5DADE2, #85C1E9)

### Technology Elements
- **Devices**: Tablets, laptops, or holographic displays
- **UI Elements**: Clean, modern interface designs
- **Visual Effects**: 
  - Soft glowing screens
  - Floating mathematical symbols or diagrams
  - Holographic projections
  - Subtle particle effects
- **Integration**: Technology feels natural and helpful, not overwhelming

### Composition & Layout
- **Format**: Landscape (1500x1060)
- **AI Position**: Left or right side, facing student
- **Student Position**: Opposite side from AI, engaged with device
- **Interaction**: Clear visual connection between AI and student
- **Background**: Simple, uncluttered, educational setting
- **Depth**: Shallow depth of field, focus on characters

### Lighting & Atmosphere
- **Lighting**: Soft, warm, inviting
- **Shadows**: Gentle, not harsh
- **Glow Effects**: Subtle emanating from AI and screens
- **Mood**: Encouraging, positive, supportive
- **Time**: Bright, daytime feel (not dark or evening)

- Strategic thinking elements
- Analytical atmosphere

- Applied mathematics focus
- Real-world problem representation
