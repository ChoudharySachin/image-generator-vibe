# CONTEXT INTRODUCTION IMAGES - STYLE GUIDE & PROMPT TEMPLATE

## Visual Style Analysis (Based on Reference Images)

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Style**: Semi-realistic, professional illustration with photographic quality
- **Tone**: Inspiring, practical, real-world application
- **Purpose**: Connect mathematical concepts to real-world contexts
- **Quality**: High-end, polished, educational

### Visual Approach
- **Realism**: Semi-realistic to realistic illustration
- **Context**: Real-world scenarios and settings
- **Integration**: Mathematical concepts visually integrated into context
- **Clarity**: Clear connection between context and learning

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Context-appropriate colors
  - Sports: Team colors, field greens, court colors
  - Nature: Natural earth tones, sky blues, greens
  - Architecture: Structural grays, blues, modern tones
  - Business: Professional blues, grays, whites
- **Mathematical Overlays**: 
  - White or light lines for diagrams (#FFFFFF, #F0F0F0)
  - Bright accent colors for highlights (#00D9FF, #FF6B6B)
  - Semi-transparent overlays for clarity
- **Background**: Context-specific, natural to the scenario
- **Contrast**: High enough for mathematical elements to be visible

### Context-Specific Elements

#### Sports Contexts (Rugby, Volleyball, Basketball, Soccer, etc.)
- **Setting**: Field, court, or playing area
- **Perspective**: Dynamic angle showing action or strategy
- **Players**: In action or strategic positions (can be silhouettes or simplified)
- **Mathematical Overlays**:
  - Trajectory lines (parabolas, angles)
  - Distance measurements
  - Angle indicators
  - Velocity vectors
  - Strategic diagrams
- **Equipment**: Balls, goals, nets visible
- **Atmosphere**: Dynamic, energetic, athletic

#### Strategy Contexts (Chess, Games, Planning, etc.)
- **Setting**: Game board, strategic overview
- **Perspective**: Top-down or angled view
- **Elements**: Game pieces, board, strategic positions
- **Mathematical Overlays**:
  - Move patterns
  - Probability indicators
  - Strategic paths
  - Decision trees
- **Atmosphere**: Thoughtful, analytical, strategic

#### Architecture/Engineering Contexts
- **Setting**: Buildings, bridges, structures
- **Perspective**: Architectural view, showing design
- **Elements**: Geometric structures, patterns, symmetry
- **Mathematical Overlays**:
  - Geometric shapes and patterns
  - Measurement lines
  - Angle indicators
  - Symmetry guides
  - Structural diagrams
- **Atmosphere**: Professional, precise, inspiring

#### Nature/Science Contexts
- **Setting**: Natural environments, phenomena
- **Perspective**: Showcasing natural patterns
- **Elements**: Fractals, spirals, patterns in nature
- **Mathematical Overlays**:
  - Pattern analysis
  - Measurement indicators
  - Geometric overlays
  - Scientific diagrams
- **Atmosphere**: Wonder, discovery, natural beauty

### Mathematical Integration
- **Overlay Style**: 
  - Clean white or bright colored lines
  - Semi-transparent when over images
  - Clear, professional diagram quality
  - Not overwhelming the context
- **Diagram Types**:
  - Angles and measurements
  - Trajectories and paths
  - Geometric shapes
  - Vectors and forces
  - Graphs and charts
- **Labeling**: 
  - Minimal, clear labels
  - Professional typography
  - Mathematical notation when appropriate
  - Arrows and indicators

### Composition & Layout
- **Format**: 16:9 (1920x1080) landscape
- **Focus**: Context as primary visual (60-70%)
- **Mathematical Elements**: Integrated overlay (30-40%)
- **Balance**: Context and math work together
- **Depth**: Clear foreground and background
- **Framing**: Professional, editorial quality

### Lighting & Atmosphere
- **Lighting**: Natural, appropriate to context
- **Mood**: Inspiring, practical, relatable
- **Quality**: High-end, polished
- **Clarity**: Clear visibility of both context and math
- **Professionalism**: Educational but engaging

## CONTEXT-SPECIFIC GUIDELINES

### Sports Contexts
**Rugby, Football, Soccer**:
- Field/pitch visible with markings
- Players in strategic positions or action
- Ball trajectory (parabolic curves)
- Distance and angle measurements
- Force vectors for kicks/throws
- Team formations (geometric patterns)

**Volleyball, Basketball, Tennis**:
- Court with net/hoop visible
- Serve or shot trajectories
- Angle analysis
- Height and distance measurements
- Optimal paths and strategies

### Strategy Contexts
**Chess, Board Games**:
- Board from strategic angle
- Pieces in meaningful positions
- Move patterns and paths
- Probability overlays
- Decision tree diagrams
- Strategic analysis

### Architecture/Engineering
**Buildings, Bridges, Structures**:
- Architectural perspective
- Geometric patterns and symmetry
- Structural measurements
- Angle and proportion indicators
- Design principles visible
- Professional, precise aesthetic

### Nature/Science
**Natural Patterns, Phenomena**:
- Natural fractals (shells, plants, etc.)
- Spirals and patterns
- Scientific measurements
- Pattern analysis overlays
- Mathematical beauty in nature
- Wonder and discovery mood
